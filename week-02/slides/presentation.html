STAGES 1 & 2

Data: Types & Sources

📊

Structured

Databases, CSV, SQL tables, spreadsheets. Fixed rows & columns. Easiest to process.

📋

Semi-Structured

JSON, XML, YAML, log files, emails. Has tags & hierarchies. Moderate complexity.

📝

Unstructured

PDFs, images, audio, video, free-form text. No fixed format. Most common, hardest to handle.

⚠️ Remember: Data scientists spend 60–80% of their time on data preparation. Good data = good AI.

STAGE 3

Model Selection Strategies

📦

Pre-Built Model

Use as-is from the catalog. Minutes to deploy. Pay per use. 90% of projects.

🎯

Fine-Tuned Model

Pre-built + your data. Hours to days. Domain specialization. ~9% of projects.

🔬

Custom Model

Train from scratch. Weeks to months. Massive data needed. <1% of projects.

💡 Rule of Thumb: Always start with a pre-built model + prompt engineering + RAG. Only fine-tune if that doesn't meet your quality bar.

Deployment Patterns

Dedicated VM. Full control. Custom scaling. Best for production & consistent workloads.

STAGE 6

Monitoring & Feedback Loops

What to Monitor

  • Performance: Latency, throughput, error rates
  • Quality: Groundedness, relevance scores
  • Safety: Content filter triggers, jailbreaks
  • Usage: Request volume, token consumption
  • Costs: Spending vs budget
  • User Feedback: 👍/👎 ratings, escalations

Feedback Loop Actions

  • Low quality? → Update data, retune prompts
  • High latency? → Scale up or use faster model
  • Safety issues? → Tighten content filters
  • New questions? → Expand knowledge base
  • Cost spike? → Optimize tokens, smaller model
  • Model drift? → Re-evaluate, retrain
🔄 Key Insight: The best AI applications have tight feedback loops. The faster you detect and fix issues, the better your AI performs.
BEST PRACTICES

MLOps — Why It Matters

MLOps = Applying DevOps principles (automation, version control, CI/CD) to the AI lifecycle.

LevelNameCharacteristics
0ManualEverything by hand, no automation
1BasicSome automated testing, manual deployment
2Intermediate ← TargetAutomated CI/CD, monitoring dashboards
3AdvancedFull automation, A/B testing, auto-scaling
4ExpertAuto-retraining, drift detection, self-healing
💡 Start simple: Version your prompts. Evaluate before deploying. Monitor in production. Add more automation as you grow.
