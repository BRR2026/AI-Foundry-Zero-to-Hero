💡 Advice for Future Learners

You have walked the path. Now share your wisdom with those who will follow. Here are common pieces of advice from graduates of intensive AI training programs:

🎓 Top 10 Tips from Zero-to-Hero Graduates

  1. Start before you feel ready. You will never feel 100% prepared — that is normal.
  2. Build things, don't just read about them. Every lab exercise is worth more than three tutorials.
  3. Embrace the confusion. If a concept is hard, it means you are growing.
  4. Use the portal first, then code. Understand the UI before automating with SDKs.
  5. Prompt engineering is a skill. It takes practice, iteration, and evaluation to get right.
  6. RAG is a game-changer. Master retrieval-augmented generation — it solves real problems.
  7. Agents are the future. Invest heavily in understanding agentic architectures.
  8. Evaluation is not optional. If you can't measure it, you can't improve it.
  9. Build in public. Share your progress on social media and blogs — accountability accelerates learning.
  10. Never stop learning. AI is moving faster than any technology before it. Stay curious.
