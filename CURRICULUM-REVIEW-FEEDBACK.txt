EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This document provides a detailed review of the proposed 45-week "Zero to Hero:
Azure AI Foundry" training curriculum. The review identifies 15 key areas for
improvement, categorized into: Wrong Platform, Wrong Placement, Missing Topics,
and Structural Issues.

Overall Assessment: The curriculum has a strong foundation and logical arc 
structure, but approximately 30% of the content covers Azure Machine Learning 
features that do not exist in Azure AI Foundry. Additionally, the most critical 
AI Foundry capabilities (prompt engineering, RAG, Prompt Flow) are either missing 
or placed too late in the curriculum.

Recommendation: Accept the structure with major revisions to Weeks 6-15 and 
reordering of Weeks 16-35.

FLAW #6: RAG Placed at Week 31 (Too Late)
Current:    Week 31 — "RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) in AI Foundry"

CATEGORY D: STRUCTURAL ISSUES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FLAW #14: No Difficulty Level Labels
Current:    All 45 weeks presented at same level
Problem:    Learners can't self-assess where to start or skip. A senior 
            developer doesn't need 5 weeks of foundations. A beginner 
            shouldn't jump to multi-agent systems.
Suggested:  Label each arc clearly:
            - Weeks 1-15:  🟢 BEGINNER
            - Weeks 16-30: 🟡 INTERMEDIATE
            - Weeks 31-45: 🔴 ADVANCED
            Add prerequisites and "skip if you know X" guidance.

SECTION 2: PROPOSED REVISED CURRICULUM (For Reference)
================================================================================

🟢 BEGINNER (Weeks 1-15)
─────────────────────────
ARC 1: Foundations (Weeks 1-5)
  1. What is AI Foundry?
  2. Understanding the AI lifecycle
  3. AI Foundry architecture & components
  4. Responsible AI principles in Foundry
  5. Setting up your environment + Hello World

ARC 2: Prompt Engineering & Models (Weeks 6-10)
  6. Prompt engineering fundamentals
  7. Model Catalog deep dive — choosing the right model
  8. Deploying & comparing models (GPT-4o vs Phi vs Llama)
  9. Fine-tuning models with your data
  10. Evaluation & testing (metrics, benchmarks, safety)

ARC 3: Data & RAG (Weeks 11-15)
  11. Connecting data sources (Blob, SQL, SharePoint)
  12. Azure AI Search & vector indexes
  13. Building your first RAG application
  14. Advanced RAG patterns (chunking, hybrid search, reranking)
  15. Evaluation datasets & quality measurement

🟡 INTERMEDIATE (Weeks 16-30)
──────────────────────────────
ARC 4: AI Agents (Weeks 16-20)
  16. Intro to AI agents & Agent Framework
  17. Building your first agent (tools, code interpreter)
  18. Multi-turn conversations & memory
  19. Custom tools & function calling
  20. Agent evaluation & testing

ARC 5: Orchestration & Integration (Weeks 21-25)
  21. Prompt Flow — visual AI orchestration
  22. Multi-model orchestration & routing
  23. Connecting to Copilot Studio
  24. Integrating with Microsoft 365 Copilot
  25. Real-world integration (Teams, Power Platform, APIs)

ARC 6: Deployment & MLOps (Weeks 26-30)
  26. Deploying model endpoints
  27. CI/CD pipelines for AI (GitHub Actions + Azure DevOps)
  28. Monitoring, observability & drift detection
  29. Scaling deployments across environments
  30. Cost optimization & quota management

🔴 ADVANCED (Weeks 31-45)
──────────────────────────
ARC 7: Security, Governance & Compliance (Weeks 31-35)
  31. Identity & access management (RBAC, Managed Identity)
  32. Data governance & Purview integration
  33. Model governance & audit trails
  34. Responsible AI dashboards & content safety at scale
  35. Securing AI workloads end-to-end (VNet, Private Endpoints)

ARC 8: Advanced Patterns & Enterprise (Weeks 36-40)
  36. Multi-agent systems & orchestration
  37. Real-time inference & streaming
  38. Building repeatable AI offerings for customers
  39. AI Foundry in CSP & managed services
  40. Enterprise case studies & partner success patterns

ARC 9: Capstone & Mastery (Weeks 41-45)
  41. Choose your capstone project
  42. Build the data + model + RAG pipeline
  43. Deploy & integrate with agents/Copilot
  44. Add governance, monitoring & security
  45. Publish your final blog: "What I Learned in 45 Weeks"
