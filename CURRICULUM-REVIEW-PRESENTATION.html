CATEGORY A

Wrong Platform — These Aren't AI Foundry Features

These 5 weeks teach Azure Machine Learning concepts that don't exist in AI Foundry.

1
Data Labeling & Annotation — AI Foundry has no labeling tool
Week 7 · Azure ML feature
2
Feature Engineering — Traditional ML concept, not applicable to LLMs
Week 9 · Azure ML feature
3
Hyperparameter Tuning — Foundry users tune prompts, not hyperparameters
Week 14 · Azure ML feature
4
Model Training from Scratch — Foundry selects & deploys, doesn't train
Week 11 · Azure ML feature
5
Data Versioning & Lineage — Purview/Azure ML, not Foundry
Week 8 · Azure ML / Purview
💡 Key Insight: AI Foundry is a GenAI/LLM platform (select → prompt → deploy → agent). It is NOT a traditional ML training platform.
CATEGORY B

Wrong Placement — Right Topic, Wrong Week

❌ Current Placement

  • 📍 RAG at Week 31 — labeled "Advanced AI"
  • RAG is used in 80% of AI Foundry apps. Teaching it at Week 31 means 30 weeks without the most common pattern.
  • 📍 Agents at Week 21
  • Agents are Foundry's flagship feature. 20 weeks is too long to wait.
  • 📍 Responsible AI only at Week 4
  • Introduced once, then not revisited for 25 weeks. Learners forget.

✅ Proposed Placement

  • 📍 RAG at Weeks 13-14 — in Beginner section
  • Learners build RAG apps right after learning about models and data. Advanced RAG patterns stay in later weeks.
  • 📍 Agents at Week 16 — start of Intermediate
  • Right after models & RAG. Natural progression.
  • 📍 Responsible AI threaded throughout
  • Intro (Wk 4) → Agent safety (Wk 17) → Deployment (Wk 27) → At scale (Wk 34)
CATEGORY C

Missing Topics — Critical Gaps

These are AI Foundry's most important features — and they're not in the curriculum at all.

✍️

Prompt Engineering

The #1 skill for AI Foundry users. System prompts, few-shot, chain-of-thought, output formatting. Not covered anywhere.

🔄

Prompt Flow

Core orchestration tool for building multi-step AI workflows visually or in code. Not mentioned.

🔍

Azure AI Search

Backbone of every RAG app — vector indexes, semantic search. No dedicated week.

🔧

Function Calling

How agents interact with APIs, databases, and tools. Fundamental but absent.

💰

Cost Optimization

Token costs, provisioned vs pay-as-you-go, quota management. Enterprise must-have.

CATEGORY D

Structural Issues

14
No Difficulty Level Labels
All 45 weeks appear at the same difficulty. Learners can't self-place or skip ahead. A senior developer doesn't need 5 weeks of foundations. A beginner shouldn't jump to multi-agent systems.
🟢 BEGINNER 🟡 INTERMEDIATE 🔴 ADVANCED
15
ARC 8 (Partner Scenarios) — Too Niche
5 full weeks on partner/CSP scenarios limits audience to Microsoft partners only. Most learners (developers, enterprise teams, students) won't benefit. Reduce to 2-3 weeks, use freed weeks for universal advanced patterns.
